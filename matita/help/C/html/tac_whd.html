whd

whd patt nodelta

Synopsis:

whd pattern [nodelta]

Pre-conditions:

None.

Action:

It replaces all the terms matched by patt with their βδιζ-weak-head normal form. If nodelta is specified, δ-expansions are not performed.

New sequents to prove:

None.
